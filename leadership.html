Meet Your Officers

William Wang

William is a rising senior who is beyond excited to serve as this year's President.

William has done well at numerous national level competitions such as PUMaC, HMMT, and MMATHS, as well as the AMC series. He qualified for the Mathematical Olympiad Summer Program (MOP) in 2019, as well as the Team Selection Test (TST) for the 2020 IMO cycle.

In his spare time, William likes to sleep, play piano, read manga, and sleep.

Eddie Chen

Vice President

Eddie is a senior who is excited to share his knowledge and wisdom as this year's Vice President. He is a six-time AIME qualifier, four-time USA(J)MO qualifier, and a top-ten finisher at MMATHS.

Other than math, Eddie also enjoys doing physics, coding, fencing, and playing the violin.

Aneesh Durai

Secretary/Treasurer

Aneesh is a senior who is excited to lead Math Club along with William and Eddie. Like the other officers, he has done very well in numerous national level competitions. He is a two-time AIME qualifier where he received a top 5% score and a top-ten finisher at MMATHS. He looks forward to guiding this year's Math Club and helping the members follow the same journey he went through.

Aside from math, Aneesh likes coding, quantitative research. reading, and learning french.
